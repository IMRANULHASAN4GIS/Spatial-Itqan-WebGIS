Import from your computer

Excel (.xlsx/.xls) & CSV — smart import: the app automatically finds your coordinate columns by any header name (Lat/Lng, X/Y, Easting/Northing, E/N…) even when they aren't side by side, detects Lat/Lng vs projected, and builds points, lines or polygons (from start/end columns, grouped vertices with an ID + order column, or a WKT/GeoJSON geometry column) — keeping all other columns as attributes. A preview lets you confirm or override before loading. Also supports Shapefile (.zip with .shp + .shx + .dbf + .prj), DXF CAD (.dxf), KMZ, KML, GPX, and GeoJSON. Files are processed locally; basemaps/search use online services.
